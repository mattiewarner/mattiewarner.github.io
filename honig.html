Honig. Chocolate is luxurious German honey chocolate. There are three different flavors, white, gold and dark. In every box, you get three solid chocolate cubes.

I designed the box based on Mies Van Der Rohe's architecture. I decided on using transparency paper to mimic Mies's love for windows in his buildings. Then I printed a dot grid on the transparency paper to incorporate Mies's grid structure.
