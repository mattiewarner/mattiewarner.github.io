Project created by:
Mattie Warner

Simp-Lici-Tea is organized around the idea of helping the environment as well as allowing you to enjoy premium ingredients, expertise, and thoughtfully crafted teas. Over the past five years, we have worked on decreasing our waste into landfills by using 100% recycled packaging and tea bags.

I wanted to show an earthy color scheme for this tea brand. I used a handwritten script with a san-serif typeface to give the brand a girlier feel. Simp-Lici-Tea has three flavors, grasshopper green, earl grey, and vanilla chai.
