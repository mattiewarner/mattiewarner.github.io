Britt's Farm is a local produce and pumpkin patch outside of Manhattan, KS. They sell local produce during their current seasons. They have a pumpkin patch during the fall and have bonfires for social events.

My design challenge was to redesign their current website. As a group, we took staged and actual photos of the site. I then wanted to create a chic rustic feel for the re-design. I choose earthy colors and simple font to create the new logo.
